About

Dormease is a web-based solution developed to streamline hostel allotment and check-in at IIT Patna. Built to address the challenges faced during campus immersion and hostel management, it simplifies the entire process through automation and real-time tracking.
This project is developed by capstone group - 14 of BSc CSDA students
Shashank Bharti (Frontend & UI/UX),
Shubham Bhatra (Backend & Database),
Richa (Operations), and
Aditya Bharadwaj (Research & Coordination)
as part of their capstone project.
